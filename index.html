TITLE: SAVING THE SACRED LOTUS FROM KALABALA

This is the storyboard of the show, designed by Yasmien.

STORYLINE

The Sacred Lotus represents unity and peace in the Hastina world. But when Kalabala, the evil magician, steals the lotus, chaos begins. Under Semar's guidance, Princess Srikandi and her brother Arjuna confront Kalabala and his traps. Together, they defeat Kalabala and bring the Sacred Lotus back to life, reviving the realm and inspiring bravery, confidence, and unity. The message of the Sacred Lotus points out the value of working together to overcome misfortune.
